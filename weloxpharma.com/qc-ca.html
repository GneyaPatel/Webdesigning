Quality Control and Quality Assurance

 

Quality control is the inspection part of quality management, whereas quality assurance is concerned with how a process or product is carried out.

Quality Control and Quality Assurance

Welox Pharma, the most reputable manufacturer and exporter of pharmaceutical products, is committed to maintaining high quality standards in order to meet the expectations of consumers throughout the world. The quality policy is implemented through a Quality Management System (QMS) that is based on strict adherence to current Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP & cGMP) in accordance with national and international regulatory standards such as WHO, FDA, and others. All of our production activities are carried out in an automated and controlled environment, with products constantly monitored.

We provide Quality Assurance (QA) through meticulous environmental monitoring, calibration, and validation systems that begin with raw material acquisition and end with final product delivery. A Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) is in place, which serves as a guideline for the procedures that must be followed for each activity performed in the plants and laboratories. The QA department works with the design, development, and production teams to maintain a quality management system throughout the firm.
