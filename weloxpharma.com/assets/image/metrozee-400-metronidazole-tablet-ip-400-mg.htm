Metrozee - 400 Metronidazole Tablet IP 400 mg

  • Product Name : Metrozee - 400 Metronidazole Tablet IP 400 mg
  • Category : Antibiotic, Antiparasitic, Antiprotozoal
  • Manufacturer Name : Welox Pharma
  • Trade Name : Metrozee 400
  • Available Dosage : 400 mg
  • Packing : Strip
  • Pack Insert/Leaflet : 1 Pack/500 Tablets
  • Ingredients : Metronidazole
  • Formulation : Tablet
  • Therapeutic use : prophylaxis and treatment of infections in which anaerobic bacteria
  • Production Capacity : 100000

Introduction to Metrozee-400:

Metrozee-400 tablets are formulated with Metronidazole IP 400 mg, a powerful antibiotic used to combat a wide range of bacterial and protozoal infections. This medication is essential for treating infections caused by susceptible organisms.


Uses of Metrozee-400:

Metrozee-400 is indicated for:


Trichomoniasis: Treats infections caused by the protozoan Trichomonas vaginalis.

Amoebiasis: Effective against amoebic dysentery and liver abscess caused by Entamoeba histolytica.

Bacterial Vaginosis: Treats bacterial infections in the vagina.

Anaerobic Bacterial Infections: Used to treat infections caused by anaerobic bacteria, such as intra-abdominal infections, skin and soft tissue infections, and respiratory tract infections.


Benefits of Metrozee-400:

Metrozee-400 offers several benefits:


Broad-Spectrum Activity: Effective against a variety of anaerobic bacteria and protozoa.

Rapid Relief: Quickly alleviates symptoms of infections, providing rapid relief.

Prevents Complications: Helps prevent the spread and complications of infections.

Well-Tolerated: Generally well-tolerated with a favorable safety profile.


Mechanism of Action of Metrozee-400:

Metronidazole: Works by entering the cells of anaerobic bacteria and protozoa. Inside the cells, Metronidazole is reduced to its active form, which interacts with the DNA of the microorganisms, inhibiting nucleic acid synthesis and leading to cell death. This action effectively stops the growth and spread of the infection.

Dosage and Administration:

Dosage: The typical dosage of Metrozee-400 varies based on the type and severity of the infection. For trichomoniasis, the recommended dose is usually 400 mg twice daily for 5-7 days. For amoebiasis, the dosage may be 400 mg three times daily for 5-10 days. Dosage for other infections should be determined by a healthcare provider.

Administration: Metrozee-400 tablets should be taken orally, with or without food. It is important to complete the full course of treatment as prescribed, even if symptoms improve before the medication is finished.
